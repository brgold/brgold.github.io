Brian R. Gold, PhD

Principal

Brian Gold has decades of strategy, market entry, competitor intelligence, and political risk analysis experience in East Asia and North America. Client projects have involved Fortune Global 500 companies, universities, non-profits, political parties, and national governments. He is an ‘alumni’ of a top five global strategy consultancy founded by Harvard Business School strategist Michael Porter. His work has been commended by the World Bank. Dr. Gold is also a historian specializing in East Asian and North American business and economic topics. He has degrees from Harvard University, the University of British Columbia, and the University of Alberta.

Kerry Sun

Consultant

Kerry Sun is a data analyst and field experiment specialist with a varied research background in neuroscience and political issues.

He has previously contributed to peer-reviewed biomedical research and was a member of the research team at the China Institute at the University of Alberta, where he helped to develop the China-Canada Investment Tracker.
