Who We Are

Brian R. Gold, PhD

Principal

Dr. Gold has decades of strategy, market entry, competitor intelligence, political consulting, and political risk analysis experience in East Asia and Canada, including over a decade based in Hong Kong and South Korea. Client projects have involved Fortune 500 Western (including Canadian) and Asian multinationals, Korean and U.S. universities, Canadian non-profits, political parties, and national governments (Western and South Korean). His policy development work for the South Korean government has been commended by the World Bank.

Dr. Gold is also a peer-reviewed published historian specializing in Korean and East Asian business and economic topics with degrees from Harvard University, the University of British Columbia, and the University of Alberta. He is a member of the Analyst Institute, a cutting edge Washington, DC based think tank for innovative voter engagement techniques. He has a working knowledge of the Korean language.

Kerry Sun

Consultant
