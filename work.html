Strategic Focus

We help organizations and business units develop a winning external strategy. An ideal strategy provides an organization with an unassailable position, one beyond maximizing operational efficiency. While operational techniques are easy to imitate, the essence of strategy is choosing a unique and valuable position rooted in a system of activities that are difficult or impossible to match.

We provide expertise in all facets of strategy, including market evaluation and entry, competitor intelligence & knowledge management, and risk analysis.

Project examples:

  • Co-leader of project for transforming East Asian nation into a ‘knowledge-driven’ economy. Key policy directives implemented nationally, commended by World Bank.
  • Successful strategic direction for client into North East Asia, including political risk advisory products involving greater China and North and South Korea.
  • Aided Western high-end chemicals producer in selecting partner for joint venture. Resulted in partnership with ‘top 3’ conglomerate subsidiary, top share of national market.

Scientific Approach

Through links with world-leading research organizations, we are contributing to an ongoing, evidence-based approach to the implementation of ‘best practices’. We conduct randomized controlled experiments that allow our clients to assess the effectiveness of a particular program or message, often challenging conventional practices.

For instance, a campaign or organization can test its direct mail on a small scale, learn from the test’s outcome, and then launch its broader mail program with confidence.

Project examples:

  • ‘First-of-kind’ field research experiment on GOTV techniques conducted during political campaign, resulting in re-evaluation of assumed sector ‘best practices’.
  • Challenged conventional knowledge of budgeting priorities for Albertan branch of major Canadian non-profit. Identified operational savings equal to 20% of yearly revenues.
